Christian Molina Mrs. Hesse English 9 13 December 2016 War On Marijuana Who cares if marijuana is legalized? Who cares if marijuana can help dying cancer patients? I believe we should legalize marijuana around the world but if we legalize it , we should legalize it for medical reasons and for recreational use. We should legalize marijuana because, believe it or not, it has some health benefits when a person smokes it. If you are having a bad day, marijuana also has many ways it can help you everyday if we legalize it recreationally. Marijuana in the past has helped people solve everyday problems . First, we should legalize marijuana recreationally for many reasons. Marijuana is proven to help people with anxiety. People get anxiety when they get nervous or stressed about something that is going to happen. Studies show that marijuana can help control certain parts of your brain that cause anxiety (“Can Marijuana Treat Anxiety Disorders?”). Studies also show that regular marijuana users that smoke this drug experience lower levels of anxiety because marijuana helps them relax.(Can Marijuana Treat Anxiety Disorders?). Marijuana should be legalized for medical reasons. There are many studies to prove how marijuana can help your health and maybe improve your health. Marijuana can help cancer patients fight cancer. In the article it says,”Scientists in the UK have now traced marijuana’s ability to prevent the growth and spread of cancer”(Studies Explain How Marijuana Cures Cancer). This means that instead of cancer growing all over the body, marijuana prevents that from happening and it kills cancer cells. When that happens, it helps the person or the patient fight cancer and it could maybe even help them beat cancer. I believe we should legalize marijuana around the world but if we legalize it , we should legalize it for medical reasons and for recreational use. People use marijuana in many ways for multiple reasons. Some people smoke this drug because they are stressed or depressed. Other people smoke it for their health, and some people smoke it to help them with their illness like cancer. People smoke this drug for medical use and most people smoke it recreationally. We should legalize this drug because of all the positives it provides us. Can you think of reasons why we shouldn’t legalize this drug?
